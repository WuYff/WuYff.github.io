I am currently a Ph.D. student working with Prof. Lin Tan in the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University. My research focuses on leveraging AI techniques to enhance software engineering (SE) practices. Recently, I work on applying and enhancing LLMs for coding tasks, includig code repair and security fixing. I also work on improving the reasoning and planning abilities of LLMs for AI agents.
