Biography

Hi, I am Yi Wu, a senior undergraduate majoring in computer science at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech). I am a member of SUSTech Automated Program RepaIr and MaintEnance Lab (A-PRIME) supervised by Prof. Shin Hwei Tan. Besides, I did a remote research internship at Software Aurora Lab in UCI under the supervision of Prof. Joshua Garcia in the summer 2020.

My research interests are in software engineering with a focus on software testing, automated program repair, and program analysis.
