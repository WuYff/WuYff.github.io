Biography

Hi, I am Haotian Ma, a third year undergraduate student of Sothern University of Science and Technology. Last year, I went to John Hopcroft Center for Computer Science as an research assistant, and got the guidance of Prof. Quanshi Zhang. Now I am a remote intern of Center for Vision, Cognition, Learning, and Autonomy.

My research interests cover from theoretical methods in deep learning to symbolic system. Particularly, I focus on interpretability of neural networks. In addition, I am interested in deep learning with symbolic based methods.
